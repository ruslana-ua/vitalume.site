Preclinical Contract Research Services for in vitro and in vivo projects

Preclinical Contract Research Services for in vitro and in vivo projects Glow Biosciences has an experienced team of scientist with strong knowledge of various animal models of disease to obtain in vivo proof-of-concept across multiple therapeutic areas.

We have many established models in Oncology, Inflammation, Autoimmune and Metabolic Diseases. We are particularly experienced with optical in vivo imaging technologies and most of our models are available with bioluminescent or fluorescent readouts.

Our AAALAC accredited vivarium and agile operational model enable quick allocation of appropriate space with technical staff to accelerate research programs.

We specialize in the following animal models:

Tumor Models

  • Breast Cancer
  • Colorectal Cancer
  • Glioma
  • Leukemia
  • Liver Cancer
  • Lung Cancer
  • Lymphoma
  • Ovarian Cancer
  • Pancreatic Cancer
  • Prostate Cancer

Other Services

  • Evaluation of drug efficacy
  • Validation of drug delivery methods
  • Organ distribution
  • Clearance
  • Target tissue level (e.g. tumors)
